## Set 1

1. The unit of electric charge is:
   - A) Ampere
   - B) Coulomb
   - C) Volt
   - D) Ohm
   - **Answer: B**

2. According to Ohm's Law, V = IR, where R is:
   - A) Current
   - B) Voltage
   - C) Resistance
   - D) Power
   - **Answer: C**

3. The opposition to current flow in a circuit is called:
   - A) Capacitance
   - B) Inductance
   - C) Resistance
   - D) Reactance
   - **Answer: C**

4. Power in an electrical circuit is given by:
   - A) P = V/I
   - B) P = VI
   - C) P = V²/R
   - D) All of the above
   - **Answer: D**

5. When resistors are connected in series, the total resistance is:
   - A) R_total = 1/(R1 + R2 + ...)
   - B) R_total = R1 + R2 + ...
   - C) R_total = R1 × R2
   - D) R_total = R1 || R2
   - **Answer: B**

6. When resistors are connected in parallel, the total resistance is:
   - A) R_total = R1 + R2
   - B) R_total = R1 × R2/(R1 + R2)
   - C) R_total = 1/R1 + 1/R2
   - D) Both B and C
   - **Answer: D**
